Network Coverage
Network Coverage with BlueCard

Preferred-Care Blue

The freedom of choice. Preferred-Care Blue® gives Blue KC members the largest selection of providers within our 32-county service area. When you want more doctors, more hospitals and more health care choices, our Preferred-Care Blue network will more than meet your expectations.

Blue Select

The model of affordability. BlueSelect offers a smaller hospital network than Preferred-Care, so it comes with a smaller price tag. This network works best if you live in the 5-county Kansas City metropolitan area. (Clay, Jackson and Platte counties in Missouri. Johnson and Wyandotte counties in Kansas.) Members living outside the metro area may enroll, but must go to a BlueSelect hospital or provider for benefits to be paid at in-network levels. Outside the 32-county Blue KC service area, BlueSelect members are covered by their BlueCard PPO.

Blue Select For Basic Individual

A smart value. This network offers even greater affordability by using a smaller hospital network limited to the Kansas City metropolitan area. BlueSelect for Basic Individual is the same 5-county Kansas City metropolitan area network as BlueSelect. But this network does not include any participating provider network out the 5-county Kansas City metro area.
