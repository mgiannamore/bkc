Wellness

The time is now to develop your own personalized wellness plan to meet your health goals. Blue KC has all the tools you need to get started on a path to healthy living.

Know Your Numbers

Schedule an annual routine physical exam with your primary care doctor to track important health measures such as blood pressure, cholesterol, and blood sugars. These exams are covered at 100 percent with a Blue KC health plan.

Know Your Risks

The Blue KC Health Risk Assessment (HRA) is a confidential, online tool that provides valuable insight into your current health status and lifestyle risks. Members can complete the HRA on their personalized member portal on MyBlueKC.com.

Know Your Plan

The personalized member portal on MyBlueKC.com also allows members to:Monitor claims activity, estimate treatment costs, read or write provider reviews, and use the many interactive health tools

Health Conditions

Are you or a family member living with a chronic condition such as asthma, diabetes or heart disease? If so, we have health advocates to support you.

Healthy Companion℠

Our Healthy Companion™ nurses can help you manage your asthma, chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD), congestive heart failure, diabetes, heart disease, high blood pressure (hypertension), metabolic syndrome, stress, anxiety and depression.

Through this program you will gain a better understanding of your health, your doctor's care plan, and how best to manage your health.

To learn more about the Healthy Companion Program call the phone number listed on the back of your Member ID card.

Illness & Surgery

Life is full of risks and uncertainties. Blue KC's team of highly-trained case management nurses are here to assist members who suffer from a catastrophic accident, high-risk pregnancy or premature birth, serious illness or surgery.

Transition to home program

With the Transition to Home Program, our nurses may visit you before you leave the hospital to help you prepare to go and stay home. The program includes follow-up support after hospital discharge for most hospitalized members.

Advanced Illness program

With the Advanced Illness Program, members and their support system are paired with a specialized nurse to provide guidance through end-of-life care and decisions.

Pregnancy

Getting ready for your new arrival can be both exciting and overwhelming. It often helps to talk with a caring professional while you are pregnant to prepare you for a healthy pregnancy.

The little starts prenatal program

The Little Stars Prenatal Program provides Blue KC members with support and resources for a safe and healthy pregnancy, including support from a prenatal nurse case manager for high-risk pregnancies.

Program forms

The following resources are for Blue KC members who are pregnant and would like to be enrolled in the Little Stars prenatal program. To get started, please fill out and submit the Welcome Assessment below. You will be directed to complete the Check In Assessment at a later time.

Behavioral Health

If you or a family member suffers from stress, anxiety, depression, alcohol or substance abuse or any other behavioral health condition, Blue KC together with New Directions Behavioral Health, an independent company that manages Blue KC’s behavioral health benefits, can help you find appropriate care. A dedicated behavioral health professional is available to you 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
