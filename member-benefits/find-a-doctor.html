HOW TO DETERMINE YOUR PHARMACY NETWORK

Locating your pharmacy network depends on whether your insurance is sponsored by your employer or you buy direct from Blue KC.

Employer Sponsored Plans with 50 or More Employees
Refer to the pharmacy section of your benefit summary. If a pharmacy network is not listed, your network is National Plus. Otherwise it is the network listed on the benefit summary.

Employer Sponsored Plans with less than 50 Employees
Refer to the back of your ID card, bottom left to determine the pharmacy network. If a pharmacy network is not on the ID card, your network is National Plus.

Individual and Family Plans
Refer to the back of your ID card, bottom left to determine the pharmacy network. If a pharmacy network is not on the ID card, your network is National Plus.

Log in to MyBlueKC.com to find a pharmacy in your network
