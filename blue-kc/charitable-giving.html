Charitable Contributions

Blue KC is committed to improving the health and wellness of the individuals and families living and working in the communities we serve. One way we fulfill this mission is through charitable contributions to a range of worthwhile causes and not-for profit organizations.

While our main charitable focus is on organizations that concentrate on health and wellness initiatives, we also give to organizations that enrich lives through social services, education, arts, culture and civic endeavors.

Qualification Musts

To be eligible for funding, you must:

  • Work to improve the quality of life and health in the community
  • Benefit communities served by Blue KC
  • Be designated by the IRS as a 501c3 charitable organization
  • Serve the community with equal opportunity regardless of age, race, sex, religion or disability

Limitations

Blue KC Does Not Consider Charitable Funding For:

  • Individuals
  • Organizations outside of Blue KC’s 32–county service area
  • Religious organizations
  • Political organizations and campaigns
  • Advertising
  • Capital campaigns

The Fine Print

Our policy is to make one contribution per year to an organization. Blue KC does not commit to multi–year funding for any organization or event. If you have multiple requests for the year, please make every effort to combine those into one funding request.
