Danette Wilson
President & CEO

Danette Wilson is President and CEO for Blue KC. She has also served as Group Executive, External Operations and Chief Marketing Executive for the last several years. Danette has worked at Blue KC for more than 25 years and has held various positions in Sales and Marketing Management prior to being appointed as a Group Executive in 2010. She is a member of the Blue Cross and Blue Shield Association Board of Directors, Kansas City Chamber of Commerce Board and the Kansas City Metropolitan Crime Commission. Danette graduated from the University of Nebraska with a Bachelor of Science degree in Business Administration.
